title: "MN 107 Ganakamoggallana: The whole path"
---

A discourse to Ganaka-Moggallana on a gradual training of a monk.


The steps laid out:

- Morality
- Sense control
- Moderation in eating
- Vigilance
- Mindfulness and consciousness
- Overcoming of the five hindrances
- Jhana
