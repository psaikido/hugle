title: "SN 2.12.2 Paticcasamuppada Vibhanga Sutta: An Analysis of Dependent Co-arising"
---

The 12 links whereby suffering arises. Each is a condition for the next to arise.

- ignorance
- volitional formations
- consciousness
- name and form
- six sense bases
- contact
- feeling
- craving
- clinging
- becoming
- birth
- suffering
