---
layout: mbm
written: 2005
title: night winds
---

<div class="poem">
night winds  
rampaging through  
this empty hotel  
try not to run  
down rattling corridors  
monsters  
kick the toilet door  
roaring  
their friends  
ransack the building  
</div>
