---
title: game
written: 2020
layout: bucket 
---

<div class="poem">
did you not see  
me tremble?  
your glance  
so piercing  

surely you saw  
that arrow hit  
its mark?  

can it be  
just a game?  
again?  
the yearning?  
the abyss?  

feel it too  
for then you would not  
hold out your arms  
and beckon me  
to jump  
</div>
