---
layout: sts
written: 2019
title: plankton
---

<div class="poem">
all over the hermitage  
polite little notices  
asking folks to be quiet  
yet showers  
and doors  
and latches  
provide opportunities  
to make them sound  
like ice hockey  
like kendo  
like gunfire


startled plankton
move further off shore
and we can't even see

the sea
</div>
