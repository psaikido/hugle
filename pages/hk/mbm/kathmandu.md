---
title: Kathmandu
written: 2006
layout: mbm
permalink: /mbm/kathmandu.html
---

<div class="poem">
rainy night in Kathmandu  
ghostly cycle rickshaw  
looms and swishes
 
rattling past
 
the whole street  
suddenly goes dark  
all but this shop
 
shining
</div>
