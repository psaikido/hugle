---
title: Leh
written: 2005
layout: sun
permalink: /sun/leh.html
---

<div class="poem">
silk route city  
our wobbly legs  
in your ancient streets
   
people step over  
a sleeping dog  
peaceful horned cow  
browsing the shops
 
we're both a bit sick  
but I feel jubilant
 
a wonderful freedom  
knowing everyone  
is me
</div>
