---
title: robin
written: 2014
layout: sun
permalink: /sun/robin.html
---

<div class="poem">
high on the rabbity downs  
in sunny seclusion  
we listened  
to the glorying mercury  
of that robin's song  


in such dazzlement  
my heart found  
its declaration to you  


a triumphant surrender  
older  
than grass  
</div>
