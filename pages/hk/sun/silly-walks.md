<div class="poem">
that wonderful dinner-time  
of anecdotes  
John told us  
of the meditation retreat  
he did with John Cleese  
from Monty Python  
I asked  
"how was the walking meditation?"  
everyone laughed  
except John who said  
"it was fine"
</div>
