---
title: Chomolungma
written: 2006
layout: sun
permalink: /sun/chomolungma.html
---

<div class="poem">
up through clouds  
sunshine flying  
we all gasp
 
Mother Goddess of the Snows  
herself  
in person
 
Makalu  
Lhotse  
Gauri Shankar
 
ok  
eyes wet  
but this time  
it's joy
</div>
