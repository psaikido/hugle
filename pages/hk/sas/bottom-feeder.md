---
title: bottom feeder
written: 2003 & 2020
layout: sas
---

<div class="poem">
bottom feeder  
only murkily aware  
of the fizz and flash  
of shoals of life  
in lighter waters  

the booming depths  
of pressured pulsing  
darkened half life  
  
yet living  

through ages  
</div>
