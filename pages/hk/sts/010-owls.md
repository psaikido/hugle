layout: sts
written: 2019
title: owls
---

<div class="poem">
deepening dusk  
hush enfolds  
last birds  
silent  

zazen expands  
boundaries blur  
distant barking  
inside  

now eternal  
night's bewitchment  
brings 

owls
</div>
