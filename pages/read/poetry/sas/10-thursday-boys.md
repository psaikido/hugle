title: Thursday Boys
layout: sas
---

<div class="poem">
up the boozer  
my mates  
the pulse of friendship  
outpacing marriages  
and careers  
  
easy come easy go  
beer and banter  
on girls  
and physics  
and  
  
  
bicycles  
bicycles  
bicycles  
</div>
